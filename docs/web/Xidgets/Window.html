Window

Overview

The window xidget is a top-level window. It may only contain a single form xidget that defines its content, and a menubar xidget. The title attribute, or child element, defines the title of the window. A window may also define the onOpen and onClose scripts for initialization and finalization.

Parameters

Title The title of the top-level window.

Scripts

OnOpen Called before the form widget becomes visible.
OnClose Called after the form widget becomes invisible.
Trigger Called when an xpath condition is satisfied after the form is visible.
